Casual Carribean Vibe by the Beach - come hang out with us! We are right off of Fort Lauderdale Beach with indoor and outdoor seating. Every evening we have a live band that plays anything and everything and Fridays are karaoke nights. South Florida's amazing reefs are just off the coast and you can see divers coming in with their catch anytime the weather permits it. Bring in your catch and we will prepare it for you any way you'd like.
